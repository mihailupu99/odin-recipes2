Glass of Water

An easy to make dish called an "Glass of Water"

A glass of water is the favorite dish at r/hydrohomies.

It is the only dish on our list that is recommended to be consumed around 2 liters a day.

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • A glass
  • WATER AGAIN THERE IS NEVER TOO MUCH WATER

Steps

  1. Carefully reach for the glass
  2. Pour the water into the glass
  3. Enjoy your freshly made glass of water
