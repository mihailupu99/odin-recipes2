Peeled Orange

An easy to make dish called "Peeled Orange"

Peeled Orange is a very popular dish that is loved by people all around the globe.

The main thing about this dish is the fact that it is very cheap and easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • Orange
  • Peels
  • Hand to eye coordination

Steps

  1. Obtain through any means an orange
  2. Carefully move your hands closer to the orange but not too fast or it will run away scared
  3. FAST FAST FAST PEEL IT
  4. (optional) Sigh in relief
